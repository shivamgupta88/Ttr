Frequently Asked Questions

How does TextToReels.in work?
Simply input your text, choose your preferences (language, platform, style), and our AI generates a professional video in seconds. Perfect for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and more.
What languages are supported?
We support 50+ languages including Hindi, English, Punjabi, Urdu, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Gujarati, Marathi, and many more regional languages.
Is TextToReels.in free to use?
We offer both free and premium tiers. Free users get access to basic features, while premium users enjoy unlimited generations, advanced AI models, and priority support.
Can I use generated videos commercially?
Yes! You own the videos generated from your text input and can use them for personal or commercial purposes, including social media marketing and content creation.
How long does video generation take?
Most videos are generated within 10-30 seconds. Complex requests with longer text or special effects may take up to 2 minutes.
What platforms are the videos optimized for?
Our AI optimizes videos for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, WhatsApp Status, Facebook Stories, TikTok, and other popular social media platforms.
